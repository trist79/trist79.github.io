GFSS

Galactic Federation of Star Systems

The UEF is proud to be a part of the GFSS in the Orion Galaxy for the past 8 years.

The Arkai Corporation

One of the two co-founders of the UEF, the Arkai Corporation represents the strongest military powers of the UEF, hosting the fleet of Cronos Megastarships

The Ombrian Empire

Also co-founders of the UEF, the Ombrian Empire strives for unity among all entities in the system through a strong government and technological innovation.

Cyprian Empire

The Empire has been pivotal to the success and growth of the UEF for over 200 years. Home planet of the UEF, Cyprus continues to be a icon of strength in the Federation.
